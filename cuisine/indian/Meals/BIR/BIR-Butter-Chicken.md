## Butter Chicken

### Overview
A rich, creamy tomato-based curry with mild spice and buttery finish. A BIR-style adaptation of murgh makhani.

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken Marinade
- 400g chicken
- 2 tbsp yogurt
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp chilli powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- Salt

### Base
- 2 tbsp butter or oil
- 1 onion (150g), chopped
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 250ml base curry gravy

### Finish
- 3 tbsp cream
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp sugar
- Â½ tsp garam masala

---

## Method

### Stage 1 Prep
1. Marinate chicken for at least 1 hour.
1. Cook (grill or fry) until lightly charred and mostly cooked.

### Stage 2 Build Base
1. Heat butter/oil.
1. Cook onions with salt until soft.
1. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook 1 minute.
1. Add tomato paste and cook until darkened.

### Stage 3 Cook
1. Add base gravy and chicken.
1. Simmer 1015 minutes.

### Stage 4 Finish
1. Add cream, butter, sugar, garam masala.
1. Simmer until rich and smooth.

---

## Notes
- Should be mild, creamy, slightly sweet
## Storage
- Refrigerate 2–3 days in airtight container
- Freeze up to 2 months; thaw fully before reheating
- Reheat gently on low heat with a splash of stock or water
- Best eaten within 24 hours for peak flavour
