## Chicken Tikka Masala

### Overview
A creamy, mildly spiced tomato-based curry using grilled or pan-fried marinated chicken, finished with a rich sauce.

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken Marinade
- 400g chicken  
- 2 tbsp yogurt  
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste  
- 1 tsp chilli powder  
- 1 tsp garam masala  
- Salt  

### Base
- 2 tbsp oil  
- 1 onion (150g), chopped  
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste  
- 2 tbsp tomato paste  
- 250ml base gravy  

### Finish
- 3 tbsp cream  
- 1 tsp sugar  
- ½ tsp garam masala  

---

## Method

### Stage 1 – Prep
1. Marinate chicken 1–24h.  
1. Grill or fry until lightly charred.

### Stage 2 – Base
1. Cook onions until soft.  
1. Add ginger-garlic paste → cook 1 min.  
1. Add tomato paste → cook until darkened.

### Stage 3 – Cook
1. Add base gravy and chicken.  
1. Simmer 10–15 min.

### Stage 4 – Finish
1. Add cream, sugar, garam masala.  
1. Simmer until thick.

---

## Notes
- Balance = creamy + slightly sweet + mild spice  

---

## Serving
Rice, naan

---

## Storage
3 days fridge
