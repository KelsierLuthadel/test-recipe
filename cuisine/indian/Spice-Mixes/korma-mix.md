# Korma Spice Mix

![Korma Spice Mix](resources/korma-mix.jpg)


## Overview
A mild, aromatic blend for creamy, nut-based curries. Emphasises warm, fragrant spices with no strong heat.

## Ingredients
- 2 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ tsp turmeric
- ½ tsp cardamom
- ½ tsp cinnamon

## Method
1. Mix thoroughly.
2. Store airtight.

## Notes
- Mild and fragrant.
- Contains no strong heat; suitable for mild palates.

## Serving
Use 1–2 tsp per korma.

## Storage
- Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place for up to 6 months
- Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture
