# Dhansak Spice Mix

![Dhansak Spice Mix](resources/dhansak-mix.jpg)


## Overview
A complex blend balancing sweet, sour, and savoury flavours, suited to lentil-based dhansak curries.

## Ingredients
- 2 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp paprika
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp mustard powder

## Method
1. Mix all spices together.
2. Store in an airtight container.

## Notes
- Works particularly well with lentils and sweet-sour profiles.

## Serving
Use 2 tsp per dhansak.

## Storage
- Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place for up to 6 months
- Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture
